## Weight Estimate Justifications

The complete system weight includes both manufacturer-documented weights and engineering estimates for components whose exact weights are not published.

The estimated values were developed using one or more of the following methods:

- Published dimensions and material-density calculations
- Documented weights of comparable products
- Known cable lengths and wire gauges
- Allowances for standard mounting and installation hardware
